Companion Model

Separate tools for local research work.

VELA can provide local workflow context for a research project. HELM reads that context and turns it into a clear dashboard. Codex remains the place where research work continues.

Codexhandoff and continuation

The repositories and responsibilities stay separate. HELM can run without VELA by showing an empty or public-safe sample state. When a VELA-compatible environment is configured, HELM reads it as a local source and shows its state.
